Human Skills

What surprised me the most about Human Skills learning is how it impacted my understanding of myself , my relationships and interactions with others, not just within my studies but in everyday life . It was actually such a lovely part to have incorporated in to our studies and curriculum because it was exactly what I needed, it was a great reminder about how important it is to look after your well being and I loved the part about Neuroplasticity. Also how emotional intelligence plays a crucial role .

What are three new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego due to the Human Skills learning?

  • Self awareness: Ive learned that this is so important in your personal life and professional settings. Understanding my strengths and weaknesses helped me to approach tasks and challenges better.
  • Empathy: Something i thought i already had lots of but after this i realised i needed to focus on having more empathy in some situations so that I can better understand others and their perspectives . This has improved my communication and listening skills .
  • Ego: I have realsied that my ego can sometimes get in the way of learning. Egos can always do with a bit of tweaking so definitely something to keep on top of.
